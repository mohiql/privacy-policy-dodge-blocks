# Privacy Policy for Dodge Blocks

Last updated: 2026

Thank you for playing Dodge Blocks.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game.

## Information We Collect

Dodge Blocks does not collect personal information from users.

The game may store limited data locally on your device such as:

* High score
* Game progress
* Settings (sound, graphics, controls)

This data is stored only on your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services provided by:

* Google Play Services

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Dodge Blocks is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

Since the game does not collect personal data, there is no transmission of personal information to external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
